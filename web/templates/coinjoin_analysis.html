‚ÑπÔ∏è About CoinJoin Detection

Detection Methods:
  • JoinMarket: Variable inputs/outputs with equal output groups meeting mathematical criteria
  • Wasabi: Multiple ~0.1 BTC outputs with coordinator fee patterns
  • Whirlpool: Fixed denominations (0.001, 0.01 BTC) with precise amount matching
Confidence Scoring:

Confidence scores range from 0-100% based on how well transactions match known CoinJoin patterns. Only transactions above 70% confidence threshold are flagged as CoinJoin.

Impact on Clustering:

Detected CoinJoin transactions are excluded from address clustering to prevent false associations between unrelated parties.
